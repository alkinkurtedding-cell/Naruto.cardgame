SHINOBI CLASH

Tactical Card Duels

Chakra regenerates each turn — spend it to play cards.

Elements counter each other for bonus damage.

Trigger a card's Jutsu when you have enough chakra for extra effects.

How to Play

Goal: Reduce your rival's HP to zero before they reduce yours.

Chakra: You start with 10 chakra and gain +3 each turn (max 10). Every card costs chakra to play — bigger cards cost more.

Clash: Pick a card from your hand. Your rival plays one too. The card with the higher ATK+DEF+PWR total wins the clash and deals damage equal to the difference.

Elements: Fire beats Wind, Wind beats Earth, Earth beats Lightning, Lightning beats Water, and Water beats Fire. A favorable matchup adds +6 to your total.

Jutsu: Every card has a unique jutsu that triggers automatically when played — extra damage, healing, shields, poison, guaranteed wins, and more.

Deck: Your hand always has 5 unique cards drawn from the full 25-character roster — no duplicates until the deck reshuffles.

Stalemate: If neither side can afford any card after chakra regen, the duel is decided by whoever has more HP remaining.
